About me

Currently delivering technical training for Generation UK & Ireland, with a focus on AWS Re/Start and the Software Engineering course. Having passed the AWS Solutions Architect Assosicate, I am currently studying for the AWS Developer Associate exam.

My greatest skill lies in the ability to take someone from absolutly nothing, to being able to write code independently. My ability to make a banging cup of tea comes a close second! Having taught myself, despite nearly failing IT at school and having dyslexia, I have developed a diverse range of approaches to suit every learner.

Other notable facts about me: My second passion to coding is CrossFit. If only I could write an algorithm to make burpees less disgusting, my life would be complete. I keep promising myself I will write a decent WOD generator. Perhaps my next project?

Attempting to smash down barriers one day at a time! A female, care experienced, first gen uni graduate who takes every 'No' as a sign that I just need to work even harder. I am extreamly passionate about challenging the lack of female, LGBTQ+ and diverse individuals in the tech sector right now! It is probably best we don't team up if anything about that statement offends...
